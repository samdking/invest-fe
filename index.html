When can I retire?

If, like me, you're not so keen on the whole retiring at 65+ life plan, you may be interested in the FIRE movement. FIRE stands for Financial Independence; Retire Early.

FIRE is the methodology whereby savings are prioritised over spending, and those savings are invested in relatively high risk/high return investments.

By the miracle of consistently saving and compound interest (your money making you more money), your investments can grow such that you can live off the interest.

This point is reached when your investments hit 25x your annual living salary. This is otherwise known as the 4% rule, where you can live off 4% of your savings a year. It's at this point you will have achieved financial independence.
