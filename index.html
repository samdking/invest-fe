When can I retire?

If you're not so keen on the whole retiring at 65+ life plan, you may be interested in the Financial Independence; Retire Early (FIRE) movement.

With FIRE, savings are prioritised over spending, and those savings are invested in relatively high risk/high return investments.

By the miracle of consistent saving and compound interest (your money making you more money), your investments can grow such that you can live off the interest.

Financial independence is considered to be when your investments hit 25x your annual living salary. This is known as the 4% rule, where you can live off 4% of your savings a year.
